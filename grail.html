Key Product

Galleri® Test

Multi-Cancer Early Detection (MCED)

The Galleri test is a breakthrough blood test that can detect a signal shared by more than 50 types of cancer. When a cancer signal is found, the test predicts where the cancer is located in the body with high accuracy.

Key Features:

  • Simple blood draw procedure
  • Screens for 50+ cancer types
  • Predicts cancer location
  • Detects cancers before symptoms
  • Complements existing screening

Important Considerations:

  • False positive and false negative results can occur
  • Does not detect all cancers
  • Not all cancers can be detected in blood
  • Requires confirmation testing if positive

Technology & Innovation

Next-Generation Sequencing

Advanced DNA sequencing technology to analyze circulating tumor DNA patterns in blood samples.

Machine Learning

AI algorithms trained on large datasets to identify cancer signals and predict tissue of origin.

Methylation Analysis

Detection of methylation patterns that are characteristic of different cancer types.

Automation

Highly automated laboratory processes ensuring consistent and scalable testing.

Clinical Applications

Early Detection

Screening for cancers before they become symptomatic, when treatment may be more effective.

Target Population: Adults aged 50 and older at elevated cancer risk

Screening Complement

Supplements existing cancer screening programs, covering cancers without recommended screening tests.

Integration: Works alongside mammograms, colonoscopies, and other standard screenings

Tissue of Origin

When cancer is detected, predicts where the cancer is located to guide further diagnostic workup.

Accuracy: High precision in predicting cancer location in the body
